Who We Are


Maccem Solutions is a multi-service construction support firm founded by Er. Meghav Gupta, a dedicated Civil engineer with both B.Tech in Civil Engineering and M.Tech in Construction technology and management. Built on the foundation of knowledge, trust, and innovation, Maccem Solutions delivers advanced services in:


  • Waterproofing Solutions
  • Tile Leakage Repair
  • Termite Control Treatments
  • Construction Consultancy
  • Greennet & Tarpaulin Sheet Supply and Installation


With a mission to solve critical construction challenges and protect valuable infrastructure, we use proven engineering techniques and modern tools to ensure reliability, cost-efficiency, and long-term durability.
