FAQs

What is waterproofing and why is it important? ▸
Waterproofing is the process of making a structure water-resistant. It protects buildings from water seepage, mold growth, and structural damage.
How do you fix tile leakage? ▸
Tile leakage is fixed by regrouting, applying sealants, and using waterproof membranes beneath the tile surface.
What is the process of termite control? ▸
Termite control involves inspection, applying anti-termite chemicals, and creating soil barriers to eliminate termite colonies and prevent future infestation.
What services are included in construction & consultancy? ▸
Our construction & consultancy services include architectural planning, structural design, material estimation, site supervision, and project execution.
What are green net and tarpaulin sheets used for? ▸
Green nets are used for scaffolding and shading. Tarpaulin sheets are waterproof sheets used to cover materials at construction sites and prevent rain damage.
