Frequently Asked Questions

What is waterproofing and why is it important? ▸
Waterproofing is the process of making a structure water-resistant. It protects buildings from water seepage, mold growth, and structural damage.
How do you fix tile leakage? ▸
Tile leakage is fixed by regrouting, applying sealants, and using waterproof membranes beneath the tile surface.
What is the process of termite control? ▸
Termite control involves inspection, applying anti-termite chemicals, and creating soil barriers to eliminate termite colonies and prevent future infestation.
What services are included in construction & consultancy? ▸
Our construction & consultancy services include architectural planning, structural design, material estimation, site supervision, and project execution.
What are green net and tarpaulin sheets used for? ▸
Green nets are used for scaffolding and shading. Tarpaulin sheets are waterproof sheets used to cover materials at construction sites and prevent rain damage.
How long does waterproofing last? ▸
Waterproofing typically lasts 7–10 years depending on the material used, weather conditions, and surface preparation quality.
Can waterproofing be done during the rainy season? ▸
It is not recommended to do waterproofing in rain. Dry conditions are essential to ensure proper bonding and effectiveness.
What causes tile leakage in bathrooms? ▸
Tile leakage is usually caused by broken grout, faulty plumbing, or absence of a waterproof membrane under the tiles.
How often should I do termite inspection? ▸
It’s advisable to inspect your property for termites at least once a year to catch infestations early and minimize damage.
Do you provide post-construction termite treatment? ▸
Yes, we offer both pre-construction and post-construction termite control services with eco-friendly chemicals and guaranteed protection.
Can you help with site estimation and budget planning? ▸
Absolutely. Our consultancy includes cost estimation, material selection, budgeting, and technical planning for all types of projects.
Are green nets reusable and UV-resistant? ▸
Yes, our green nets are durable, UV-resistant, and can be reused multiple times at construction and agricultural sites.
What sizes of tarpaulin sheets are available? ▸
We offer tarpaulin sheets in various sizes, ranging from small covers to large sheets used for trucks and warehouses.
Do you offer free site visits before providing services? ▸
Yes, we provide free site inspections and assessments before preparing a custom service plan and quotation.
Is there any warranty on your services? ▸
Most of our services come with a warranty period ranging from 1 to 5 years, depending on the type of work and materials used.
